The Development Dialogue (DD) is the agreed system for performance contracting, management and development at UCT for permanent professional, administrative and support services (PASS) staff in pay classes 1 to 12. From 1 June 2015, this replaces the previous Personal Performance System (PPS). View the Development Dialogue resource guide for more information.
