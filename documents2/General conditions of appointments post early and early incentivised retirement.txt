2.2.1. The appointment of an individual after early or incentivised early retirement is possible subject to the following conditions:

The contract will not extend beyond the year in which the individual turns 65.
The appointment is subject to normal recruitment and selection criteria including EE criteria.
Conditions of service pertaining to the contract will be adhered to. This includes contributions to the UCTRF where included in the conditions of service.
Any medical aid subsidy will not be awarded during such time as the 'retiree' is employed full time at UCT. When returning to the status of 'retiree', award of the medical aid subsidy as at time of retirement will resume.
