1.3.1. Early retirement arises when, for operational and/or transformational reasons, a Head of Department may apply for the incentivised early retirement of a staff member, with the support of the staff member. This can take place any time after the staff member attains the age of 55 and before the year in which they turn 65.

1.3.2. A package is negotiated with the EDHR, and is subject to the approval of the Vice-Chancellor and the Remuneration Committee of Council.

1.3.3. Reasons for incentivised early retirement include:

To facilitate the reorganisation of an area or unit; and/or
In order to retain or advance staff who are promotable; and/or
To advance an employment equity plan, or for employment equity reasons.
